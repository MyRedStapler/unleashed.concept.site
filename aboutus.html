Unleashed is a high-energy, dance rock band performing covers of favorite classic rock songs from the 70’s through the 90’s and beyond! Fronted by Nena French’s powerful vocals and captivating stage presence, Unleashed delivers memorable performances to audiences all over northern California. Drummer Charlie McGimsey and bassist Jim Cobb form one of the best rhythm sections in California and lay down the groove for the band’s infectious sound. Add the sonic textures and vocal harmonies of keyboardist Barry Boudreau and guitarist Forrest Walsh, and you have a talented band that consistently delivers an amazing experience for their audiences.
